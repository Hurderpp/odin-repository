Grilled Cheese

Description

 A simple dish, yet one that still remains quite filling, especially as a nighttime snack.

Ingredients
  • Slices of Bread
  • Cheese, preferably American Kraft
  • Butter (optional)

Steps
  1. Heat up pan, then place butter.
  2. Place cheese between slices of bread.
  3. Place sandwich on pan.
  4. Let grill for approximately 2 min, then flip
  5. Remove grilled cheese sandwich from pan and serve!
